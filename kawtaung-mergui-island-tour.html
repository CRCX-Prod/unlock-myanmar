Kawtaung - Mergui Islands by Boat

If you are wondering where is the best untouched beaches and Islands to relax before your end of the trip in Myanmar? 

Here we go!!!  We recommended Kawthaung - which is the most southern part of town in Myanmar and from there you can access to a lot of fabulous islands by boat. It is part of Myeik Archipelago which has 

These islands are recently developed a few years ago and people visit here to do diving & snorkeling & chills at the beach.

Kawthaung is our first trip ever arranged by Unlock Myanmar “Snorkeling Tour “for our guests and THEY LOVE IT.

We can offer you: 

  • 3 Nights 4 Days Tour
  • 4 Nights 5 Days Tour
  • 5 Nights 6 Days Tour depend on your request

Flight schedule to Kawthaung is very limited and sometimes only one flight in a day.The boat trip starts 8:30 am in the morning and if your flight schedule in the afternoon , we arrange boat trip in next day. 

Standard 3 Nights 4 Days Tour

Day 1 – City Tour or Chill at the Hotel 

 When you arrive in Kawthaung you have option like you can visit city tour or you can relax at your hotel doing nothing the whole day. During the city tour, you are visiting to the attraction of the town places like - 

  • Bayint Naung Point (Victoria Point)
  • Bayint Naung Monument Square
  • Pyi Daw Aye Pagoda
  • Ngar Thone Lone Hill (555 Hill)
  • Pulon Tontone Beach and Bridge
  • Maliwon Waterfall and Hot Spring
  • City Market

Day 2 – Boat Trip

  • Depart at 08: 30 at the jetty
  • Visit beautiful islands by speedboat
  • Snorkeling, swimming around different islands, chilling out and taking photos
  • Lunch at island on the beach food, fruit and soft drinks served
  • Afternoon snorkeling around the islands
  • Overnight in tents with sweet dreams at Satan Island
  • Dinner served on Satin Island


Day 3 – Boat Trip

  • Breakfast at the tent on the beach
  • Snorkeling, swimming, visiting islands around the sea in a small boat
  • Lunch on the island 
  • Leave from the island at 1 pm after lunch 
  • Back to Kawthaung in the evening around 3:30 pm
  • Then we transfer to hotel 
  • Relax at the hotel in the evening time

Day 4 – End of the trip

  • Relax and swim at the hotel in the morning
  • Depart to the airport in the afternoon
  • The end of an amazing, memorable experience

You have many options to choose which island you would like to visit and how long because they are over 800 islands. And we can advise you for that. 

We are offering day return boat trip and overnight boat trip tours. You can do island hopping in one day or stay a few days more to chill at the beach.

Lampi Island Tour (Marine National Park) – Day Trip 


Lampi Island, the only one of the marine National Park in Myanmar which is the most famous island in Mergui Archipelago. Many kinds of wildlife can see on this island. Moreover, there were various kinds of plants also growth on this island. There are more than three Mangrove rivers are situated on this island. 


  • Leave at 6 am in the morning to the village by car (approx. 1 & half hour) 
  • Boat trip around 2 and half hour to reach Lampi Island 
  • Visit around Lampi Island (swim, snorkeling & Chill)
  • Visit some villages near Lampi island 
  • Back to the village by boat at 3 pm in the afternoon 
  • Dinner on the way to town and back to hotel

The exact trip will be tailored to your wishes and interest, dependent on your time available and budget.

Includes all transfers, transportation, accommodation, entrance and guide fees, snorkeling equipment, buffet lunch, snack and life jacket.
