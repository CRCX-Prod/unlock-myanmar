Yangon city life

Overview

Exploring Yangon is taking you to the sights of former British colonial city known as Rangoon. On the trip we are taking a walk to visit to see people living on and around of Yangon and learning about the history of colonial architecture and buildings, hidden down side streets, biggest concentration is on the river front Strand road, around the bottom of Pandsodan street, where you will see the local Myanmar people lifestyle. 


Are you ready to start your day? Let’s Unlock Yangon together – let’s go!


We will start sightseeing, we will start walking from Sule Pagoda, City Hall, Independent Monument and continue to see Colonial buildings, hidden down side streets, biggest concentration is on the riverfront Strand road, around the bottom of Pansodan street, You will see the local Myanmar people lifestyle in just one day!


Now of course, it is turn to learn about Tea! Tea is a part of Myanmar culture and important in daily life of Myanmar.We have tea for breakfast, lunch and dinner and tea shops are in the version of western bars in local people life.


Lunch time!! Let’s try some tasty and delicious traditional Burmese food an after that we visit to 

 Shwedagon Pagoda which is one of the must go when you are in Myanmar. Your mind will be peaceful with fresh air and cool breeze. What a beautiful end to a full day!
