3 Ancient Cities Tour

Three Ancient Cities Tour 

We are organizing daily tours and One of them is popular Three Ancient Cities in One Day Culture Tour in Mandalay.

On this trip we’re going to ancient, Super ancient - Amarapura, Sagaing, and Inwa—three former capitals that you’ll get to see in one jam-packed day of pagodas, ruins and markets. Think of it as a crash course in Myanmar history and culture! 


  • Amarapura: The interesting place which become the center of Buddhism reforms and learning. You will see a lot of famous sacred shrines, pagoda, monasteries, traditional workshops there.
  • Mahar Myat Muni Pagoda
  • Mahar Gandar Yone Monastery
  • Silk Waving 

  • Sagaing: One of the Myanmar’s ancient capitals, famous for its many hundreds of white, silver and gold pagodas and monasteries and its hilly landscape. 
  • Sagaing Hill
  • Sone Oo Pone Nya Shin Pagoda
  • 30 Cave Pagoda
  • Lunch break in Sagaing
  • Inwa (Ava): The capital of Myanmar kingdom for more than six hundred years. You will see the pagodas which are similar with Bagan and one of the pagoda “Yandanar Simi Pagoda is famous for “Pagoda Lost in the Garden”. Inwa is named after like” Mini-Bagan or Little Bagan”

  • Maha Aungmye Bonzan Monastery (Mal Nu  Monastery) 
  •  Bargayar Monastery (Teak-constructed Monastery)
  • Yadanar Semi Pagoda

  • U Being Bridge: The best place to enjoy the sunset in Mandalay!
