Trekking in Chin

Overview

Trekking in Chin State will take you the discovery of the lost tribe and the story of “

The Face- Tattooed Women in Chin State “ - their living culture, way of living and way of thinking. This trip is perfect for trek lover who love the challenging trek. This trekking tour is leading you to an adventurous trip finding out the fascinating mountain views, enjoyable scenery crossing the Chin mountains and learning about the local ethnic.


Day 1

Travel from Bagan and arrive at Kampetlet in the afternoon. Meet with your guide to explain you an overview of the trip plan. 

Day 2

Start your trekking to Htar Shwe village and you will learn the way of life of local people and you can learn more when you are doing home stay. Overnight in Htar Shwe 

Average walking hours is 6 to 7 hours but we have motorbike stand by.


Day 3

Trekking for the short walk to Kyar Bo village where you can see the different traditional arts including facial tatoos. Then you continue trekking before lunch break. From Kyar Bo you’ll continue on to Aye camp for a quick lunch break. After lunch you’ll cross the Chi River on your way to Luke Dee village. Spend your night in Luke Dee with a local Chin family. 


Day 4

The next morning, it’s time to hike out and you’ll leave Luke Dee village and walk down to Mindat. Enjoy your great meal in Mindat. After lunch, explore around Mindat. Overnight in Mindat


Day 5

Breakfast at the hotel and you can begin your journey back to Bagan.

End of memorable trip!


Trip Includes

All transfers, transportation, accommodation, entrance and guide fees, homestay fee, lunch, dinner, motorbike, breakfast

 

Trip Outline

The exact trip will be tailored to your wishes, dependent upon your time available and desired budget.
