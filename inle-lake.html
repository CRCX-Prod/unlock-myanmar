Classic Cultural

Overview

The classic tour of Myanmar taking in the unmissable highlights of the country, including the ancient temples of Bagan, the serenity of Inle Lake and Mandalay, the last kingdom of Myanmar. 

Day 1

Exploring the surroundings of Mandalay, exploring its history, visiting the Three Ancient Cities of Sagaing, Amarapura and Ava (Innwa), ending the day with sunset at the famous U Bein bridge (the oldest teak bridge in the world).

Day 2

Take the boat along the Irrawaddy river to the magnificent ruin of Mingun, the towering 18th century temple overlooking the river, the nearby 90-ton bell from that era and the stunning and brilliantly white Myatheindan Pagoda.  

Back tom Mandalay, the tour will take you to the “Kuthodaw Pagoda”, fondly known as the world’s largest book, as well as the Golden Palace Monastery. The day will end at Mandalay Hill for sunset and the stunning view over the city.

Day 3

Drive to Bagan talking in the rainy season greenery of the Myanmar dry zone, arriving in time for a late lunch. Exploring the surroundings of Bagan by car.

Option (Boat – from Mandalay to Bagan) leave from the jetty at 7 am and will arrive Bagan before sunset.

Day 4

Visit Bagan ancient pagodas the whole morning time and in the evening around 4 pm, you will visit to Peter’s garden and you will visit the sunset boat tour on the Irrawaddy river. Enjoy the sunset at the boat and transfer back to the hotel.

Day 5

Start the day with Inle boat trip and head across the lake to the market village and we’ll take you by boat to Indein Village. Walking around the village passing the paddy fields where the farmers are doing plantations and visit the ancient buddha images on the hilltop pagoda and manufacturing workshops in the lake and see the amazing Inle one legged fishing style. Visit to the long neck tribal women of their waving style in the village and back to hotel. 

Day 6

Visit to Pintaya Pagoda which is 2 hour drive from Inle – Nyaung Shwe and driven back to the Red Mountain Vineyard to enjoy the sunset. Then come back to hotel and enjoy your own relaxing evening. 

Day 7

Enjoy your own activities in the morning and travel back to Yangon by flight (Transport option: by bus, flight, private car) 

End of a Memorable trip!
