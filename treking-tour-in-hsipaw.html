Treking in Hsipaw

Tour – Hsipaw

Hsipaw is a loving town which is in the northern part of Shan state.

 If you are looking for trekking experience in Myanmar – Hispaw is one of the best treks recommend to do!

Our experienced local English guide is ready to accompany with you in your adventure. 

During the trek, you will walk through the farmland, hills and jungle passing through bamboo forests and will get a great experience staying with the local family in the rural village like a local person. Food and accommodation are organized in villagers' houses. 

 A good chance to learn about the simple lifestyle of local people, clean air and fresh organic food from the farm and trees- Shan food is so good & delicious.

We can offer you:

Half Day Tour/ Full Day Tour 

  • Nam Tuk Gyi waterfall, Shan Villages & Par Pheik Hot Springs
  •  Little Bagan, Shan Village along the river (Boat Tour) 

You can go for a few hours hike or combine hiking with a boat on the river


Overnigh Treks

  • 1 Night 2 Days Trek
  • 2 Nights 3 Days Trek
  • 3 Nights 4 Days Trek

The mountains surrounding Hsipaw are home to the Palaung people, whom the legend says descend from dragons. During the overnight treks in Hsipaw you can observe the tea cultivation and their traditional way of life.

The exact trip will be tailored to your wishes and interest, dependent on your time available and budget.

Includes all transfers, transportation, accommodation, entrance and guide fees.
